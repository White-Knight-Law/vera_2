Privacy, limits, and safe use

This website is not a secure case-management portal. Information entered into the site may be accessible to anyone who can access this browser profile or device.

Where information is stored

Intake answers, timeline events, and evidence inventory entries are stored in the browser using local storage. The website does not send this information to WhiteKnight Law, an advocate, an attorney, law enforcement, or any other person.

Do not enter

  • Passwords, one-time codes, or recovery codes
  • Social Security numbers or government identification numbers
  • Full bank, card, or financial account numbers
  • Intimate images or recordings
  • Medical records containing unnecessary identifiers
  • Information that could increase danger if another person sees it

Legal and professional limits

VERA is not a lawyer, law firm, police agency, therapist, investigator, medical provider, or emergency service. It does not determine whether a crime occurred, assess credibility, identify a guilty person, guarantee confidentiality, create legal privilege, provide legal advice, or establish an attorney-client relationship.

Delete information from this browser

This removes VERA information stored by this website from the current browser. It does not delete downloaded files, printed pages, browser backups, screenshots, or information copied elsewhere.

VERA is an organizational victim-support tool. It does not replace emergency services, a trained victim advocate, licensed attorney, medical care, mental-health care, or law enforcement.
